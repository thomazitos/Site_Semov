Descrição

Pursuing a career in risk analysis -and enjoying it- was motivated in part by the realization that if risk perception was becoming reality, we had to inject some reality in the perception! A major challenge was recognizing, assessing and communicating uncertainties. Another one was to connect the analysis and the failure risks of a given system with the human and management factors that affected its different parts. I will present briefly a few examples in very different fields: the heat shield of a spacecraft, the risk to anesthesia patients, and the cyber risk of different organizations.

Palestrante

Elisabeth Pate-Cornell

A Dra. Marie-Elisabeth Paté-Cornell é professora na escola de engenharia e professora e fundadora da cadeira do departamento de Ciências de gestão e Engenharia da Universidade de Stanford. A sua especialidade é a análise de risco de engenharia com aplicação a sistemas complexos (espaciais, médicos, plataformas petrolíferas offshore, etc.). A sua pesquisa anterior centrou-se na optimização de sistemas de alerta e na inclusão explícita de factores humanos e organizacionais na análise dos riscos de falha dos sistemas.
